Privacy Policy

myQuran No Personal Data Collected
Last updated: September 30, 2025

myQuran is developed and maintained by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

Information Collection and Use

  • The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.
  • No registration, login, analytics, or advertising SDKs are used.
  • No personal data is transmitted to third parties.

Permissions Used

The app may request the following Android permissions to support core functionality:

  • Internet Access — to fetch Qur’an text, Maranao translation, and Tafsir content.
  • Notifications (optional, future feature) — to send daily Ayah reminders if enabled by the user.
  • Storage/Media (optional, future feature) — to enable offline saving of content, if implemented.

These permissions are used solely to deliver app features. The app does not access contacts, location, camera, microphone, SMS, or call logs.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Because the app does not collect or store personal data, risks related to unauthorized access to personal information are minimized.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or payment processors.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this page/URL with an updated “Last updated” date.

Contact
